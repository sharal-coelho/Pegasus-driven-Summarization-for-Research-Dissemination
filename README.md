The scientific research process begins with investigating the state of the art, which may involve a vast number of publications.

Summarization is the process of reducing a source text into a shorter version while preserving its information content. 

Automatically summarizing scientific articles helps researchers in their investigation by speeding up the research process.

The shared task Research Highligh tGeneration from Scientifc Papers(SciHigh) focuses on automatically generating research highlights from scientifc paper abstracts.
